## How to use this file

Before writing any scene, the chapter-generation skill follows three steps:

1. Read this file in full.
2. Choose scene locations from this file. Note their adjacencies and travel times.
3. Write the scene using only these chosen locations. When characters move, use the adjacency notes to keep distances and routes consistent.

If a chapter genuinely needs a location not in this file, the chapter cannot proceed. The user must manually add the location first.

## HIGHLAND DISTRICT (north of Halford Avenue)

### Vance Estate — exterior grounds
Forty acres of land in the Highland District. Includes the stone main house, formal gardens around the south side, an extensive lawn rolling east toward the woodland, a stocked pond with herons, stables (not in active use), and the private woodland that wraps the north and east edges of the property. Connects internally to all parts of the main house. Connects externally to the long private drive that meets the public road. The woodland on the property is continuous with Roman's regular running grounds.

### Vance Estate — east terrace
A stone terrace on the east side of the main house. Roman takes his morning coffee here. Connects to the breakfast room through French doors and to the east lawn down three stone steps. *Used in Ch 1 and Ch 200 for the circular structure.*

### Vance Estate — entrance hall
Formal stone entry, broad staircase to the upper floor, dim during the day. Connects to the main hall, the formal dining room, and the front porch.

### Vance Estate — kitchen
Large, professional-grade. Roman cooks here. Connects to the breakfast room, the side garden door, and the staff corridor. The kitchen is where Elena first watches Roman cook in Arc 1.

### Vance Estate — breakfast room
Smaller dining space adjacent to the kitchen. Connects to the kitchen and the east terrace.

### Vance Estate — formal dining room
Used rarely, for visiting Alphas and large pack receptions. Connects to the entrance hall and a serving corridor to the kitchen.

### Vance Estate — Roman's study (the locked office)
Roman's private study within the main house. Holds a desk phone Elena identifies in Arc 2. Always locked when Roman is not present. Connects to the upstairs hallway.

### Vance Estate — Roman's bedroom
Shared with Elena from Arc 4 onwards. Large room with a fireplace, an east window onto the pond, and a connecting door to Roman's private bathroom. Connects to the upstairs hallway.

### Vance Estate — Roman's bathroom
Adjacent to Roman's bedroom. Includes a deep tub (site of the bubble bath touch scene, Arc 9) and a large shower (site of the bond night touch scene, Arc 19).

### Vance Estate — Elena's original guest room
Used Arcs 1–4. Down the hall from Roman's bedroom. Has its own en-suite bathroom.

### Vance Estate — woodland edge
The line where the lawn ends and the private woodland begins. Where Elena enters the woods at the start of her escape attempt in Arc 4. Roman's regular running grounds extend from this edge through the full woodland and connect to the wilder forest beyond the estate boundary.

### Vance Estate — pool
Outdoor pool on the south side of the grounds, with a sunset view. Site of the pool touch scene in Arc 9.

### Vance Estate — balcony
Overlooks the estate grounds from the upper floor. Elena waits on this balcony for Roman to return in Arc 15. Connects to Roman's bedroom and the upstairs hallway.

### Helena's dowager estate
Twenty minutes outside the city, on a smaller property. Helena moved here after the old Alpha died. She lives alone with staff who come in. Elena does not visit here in the main arc.

### Cassia's penthouse
Central Highland District. Not adjacent to Vance Estate — a 20-minute drive in Highland traffic. Werewolf-social space. Cassia hosts gatherings here for her circle. Modern, top-floor, expansive.

### The lake house
Roman's, two hours' drive from the city. Private dock, isolated. Site of the courting weekend in Arc 4.

### The Wildwood Club
Members-only werewolf-women's social and athletic club. Clubhouse with yoga studio, dining room, lounges, and kennels for shifting before runs. Two hundred acres of attached forest the women run in. At the outer edge of Highland; thirty minutes by car from Vance Estate. Cassia is a long-standing member. Elena teaches the yoga group here starting in Arc 8.

### The lingerie boutique
Exclusive Highland boutique that does not normally serve humans (Elena enters as Roman's mate). Quiet, expensive, with several private fitting rooms. Site of the boutique touch scene (Arc 9, scene 4). In central Highland.

### The werewolf bar
Upscale Highland-side bar with a dance floor. Site of the bar touch scene (Arc 9, scene 9). About fifteen minutes from the Wildwood Club, twenty minutes from Vance Estate.

### The werewolf resort
Mountain setting, several hours' drive out of the city. Visiting Alphas from other territories come here for events. Roman's birthday and the midpoint Arc 7 events take place here. Stays involve at least one overnight.

## CITY CENTRE (downtown)

### Roman's downtown Alpha office
Roman's executive office for territorial matters. Within a building near the Alpha Council Building complex. Site of Holloway's office tantrum (Arc 11) and the lap-straddle touch scene (Arc 9 scene 8). Walking distance to the Council Building (about 5 minutes).

### The Alpha Council Building complex
Multi-building government complex containing the council chamber, individual Alpha offices, committee rooms, antechamber, press steps, and the records vault. The records vault is the target of Diego's documents heist (Arc 14). The complex is heavily guarded. Site of the Council Conference (Arcs 10, 13, 14).

### The press steps
Outside the Council chamber. Press wait here for Alphas exiting after sessions. Slow-burn touch scene 2 (Arc 9, the car scene with oral) takes place in a parked car at the foot of these steps with cameras waiting.

### The dowager tea room
Helena's favoured Highland-edge cafe. Quiet, traditional, the kind of place that doesn't serve people without standing. Helena Visits 1 and 2 (Arcs 2 and 4) involve tea here.

## SOUTH SIDE (south of Halford Avenue)

### Halford Avenue
The boundary between Highland (north) and South Side (south). A four-lane road. Crossing it is a sensory event — the air changes, the smells change, the light feels different. 25-minute drive from Vance Estate to the South Side core (the area around Cruz Community Fitness).

### Cruz Community Fitness
Storefront gym on Hidalgo Avenue, South Side. Bright yellow exterior. Two studio spaces (pre/post-natal yoga in one, HIIT in the other), a small free-weight area, an office at the back. Elena's business. Used in Ch 2, Arcs 17 and 18, and the final scene at Ch 200 (looking out from the gym to the completed sports ground).

### The empty lot / future sports ground
Next door to Cruz Community Fitness, on Hidalgo Avenue. Empty for years. The neighbourhood community centre next door closed two years ago due to funding cuts. The lot is the subject of Elena's three-year petition. **In the final scene (Ch 200) it is the completed sports ground — children playing, Elena standing with Roman, pregnant.**

### Cruz Bakery (Marisol's)
Marisol's small Mexican bakery. Two doors west of the gym on Hidalgo Avenue. Marisol works long hours here. Roman meets Marisol in Arc 17 — the meeting happens at the Cruz family home, not the bakery, but the bakery is referenced and visible during Roman's South Side visit.

### The Cruz family home
A modest South Side house, several blocks from Hidalgo Avenue. Where Roman has dinner with Elena's family in Arc 17. Marisol's home. Diego and Elena grew up here.

### Diego and Lucía's home
A separate South Side residential block, several blocks from the family home and the gym. Where Diego POV chapters take place. The kitchen is where Lucía has the recurring *why do you bother* arguments with Diego. Mateo's wooden lion lives on the table here. Modest. Lived-in. A backyard with a gate that needs fixing.

### The South Side Hearing Hall
Squat municipal building at the corner of Hidalgo and Vega. Where chapters 1–9 took place. Fluorescent lighting, folding chairs, scuffed linoleum. Not used after the post-Ch-9 arc except by reference.

### Detention facility (city outskirts)
Where Diego is held in Arc 16. Cold, fluorescent, processed. Standard pack-administered detention. Site of Elena's visit to Diego after his arrest.

### The small government building Diego's cell burns
A minor administrative building in the city, somewhere between South Side and the downtown core. Site of Diego's first successful action (referenced in Arc 9 Diego POV). The building itself is not on page; the action is shown from Diego's POV.
