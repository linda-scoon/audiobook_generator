## What the tiers mean in practice

### Tier 0
- The chapter contains no sexual or romantic physical contact.
- Tension is allowed and encouraged through proximity, scent, possessive gestures, the feeding ritual, the wolf's responses.
- Roman's wolf may react to her physically (the lean, the pull) but no acted-on intimacy.

### Tier 0.5
- Spooning (Roman holding Elena while sleeping).
- Holding, hugging, forehead touches, the wrist-to-pulse-to-nape tracing in moments of high emotion.
- Hand on back, hand at her elbow, fingers in her hair.
- No kissing on the mouth. No deliberate sexual touch.

### Tier 1
- All of Tier 0.5 plus:
- Kissing — mouth, neck, shoulders, hands, anywhere.
- Breast contact — through clothes and bare. Suckling.
- Fingering.
- Oral sex on Elena.
- Hand jobs.
- Foot, toe, leg suckling.
- Clothed friction including to climax.
- **Line not crossed: penetrative sex.** No PIV on page. No oral on him on page.
- Wait — clarification: oral on Roman happens in Arc 19 (Elena initiates in the shower). For Tier 1 the boundary is *no completed sex acts on page*. Roman receiving oral happens only in the explicit chapter (Tier 2).
- After-marks on bodies, breathlessness, sweat, ruined clothes — all fair game inside Tier 1.

### Tier 2 — Arc 19 only
- Open door.
- Penetration on page.
- The bite (Roman's teeth, Elena's neck or shoulder, the bond sharing).
- Multiple acts across multiple locations through the house.
- **Critical:** Elena's consent to the bite is on page. Roman asks (*can I?* or equivalent). Elena says yes. The bite follows.
- Cool-down after the act — afterglow, fragmentary memory references, the marked body, the bonded feeling that floods Elena for the first time.

### Tier 0 — chapter 200
- No physical heat in the final chapter.
- The east terrace, the ring, the kneeling. Then the cut to the sports ground.
- The book closes on emotional culmination, not physical.

## The line that must not be crossed early

**Penetration belongs in Arc 19 only.** Not in flashforward, not in dream sequence, not in flashback (the bond is shared via the act, so it cannot have occurred earlier — there is no "earlier" PIV scene in this story). The slow-burn thread is Tier 1 throughout. The bond night is the only chapter range that contains the act.

---

## The bite — special handling

The bite is the moment the bond shares. It is on page in Arc 19. It requires Elena's explicit consent within the act. The chapter prose should:

- Show Roman asking. Short. Maybe one line. *Can I?*
- Show Elena consenting. Short. *Yes.*
- The bite follows.
- The bond shares in the next breath.
- Elena's experience of the bond rushing in is a major sensory event — show it. The pull she could not feel for 195 chapters is suddenly there. Disorienting, then overwhelming, then settling.

The bite is not before the consent. The bite is not after a pause where she could have stopped him. The structure is: ask → answer → bite → bond.
