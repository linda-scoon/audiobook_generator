## Identity
- **Full name:** Mateo Cruz
- **Title/Role:** son of Diego and Lucía Cruz. Nephew of Elena Cruz. The smallest figure in the story; the reason for everything Diego decides.
- **Age at story start:** 6 or 7 (use 6 unless a chapter specifies otherwise).

## Physicality
- Small for his age. Skinny.
- Dark hair, big dark eyes.
- Warm brown skin.

## Family
- Son of Diego and Lucía Cruz.
- Nephew of Elena Cruz.
- Grandson of Marisol Cruz.

## Personality
- Six. Curious. Adores his Aunt Elena.
- Asks where she is repeatedly across the book once she has gone.
- Has a wooden lion Diego carved for him at age 3. The mane was painted yellow; the paint is wearing off. The lion goes everywhere with him.

## Speech
- Six-year-old speech. Simple, direct, sometimes painfully honest.
- He calls Elena *Tía Elena.*

## Function in the story
- Mateo is the reason Lucía pushes back on Diego's activism.
- Mateo is the reason Diego pushes forward.
- Mateo is a small, quiet weight in every Diego POV scene.
- He should be present (even just by reference — the lion on the table, the bedroom door closed) in most Diego POV chapters.
