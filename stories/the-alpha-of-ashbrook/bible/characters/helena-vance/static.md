# HELENA VANCE — STATIC BIBLE

> Permanent facts about Helena Vance.
> Read-only after Step 3b.

---

## Identity
- **Full name:** Helena Vance
- **Title/Role:** Dowager of the Vance Pack. Mother of Roman and Cassia. Widow of the late Alpha Vance. Recurring presence in Elena's life across the book. Two dedicated POV chapters in Arcs 8 and 9.
- **Age at story start:** early 60s.

## Physicality
- Around 5'7".
- Slender, elegant.
- Silver-grey hair (was dark in her youth).
- Olive skin, light wrinkles at the eyes and mouth.
- Pale grey eyes — Roman inherited from her.

## Wolf form
- Smaller than her children's wolves but distinguished. Silver-grey.

## Family
- Widow of the late Alpha Vance, who died ten years before story start (natural causes).
- Mother of Roman (38) and Cassia (32).
- No other living siblings or close family on page.

## Position
- The Dowager of the Vance Pack. The position carries social weight in pack society — she is the matriarch of the family, even though Roman is the Alpha and runs the territory.
- Lives at her own dowager estate twenty minutes outside the city. Moved here after the old Alpha died and Roman took over the main hold.
- Maintains a wide social circle in Highland.
- Politically traditional but not hardline.

## Personality
- Quietly powerful.
- Watchful. She measures every room she enters.
- Dignified. Has spent her whole life in a position of social visibility and has learned to carry it.
- Slowly hardening across the book — her arc is from absorbing slights to refusing them.
- Loves her son fiercely. Loves Elena as her daughter-in-law once she has met her.

## Speech patterns
- Measured, formal in public, warmer in private.
- Does not curse.
- Uses formal pack vocabulary by default.

## Internal voice (when her POV)
- Dignified, watchful, slowly hardening.
- She is old enough to remember when the world was smaller for her too — she has internalised many of the things she is now learning to refuse.

## Position on Elena
- Will meet Elena early (Arc 2 — Helena Visit 1, taking her out for tea).
- Will continue to visit through the book, with shifting access as Roman's possessiveness adjusts.
- Will intervene in Arc 8 with the *she is not a prisoner* emotional appeal.
- Will openly defend Elena in pack society by Arc 17.

## Position on werewolf society's treatment of humans
- Has absorbed many small bullying interactions across her life — werewolves who looked down on her associates, her staff, her tradesmen.
- Was passive about it for most of her life.
- The arrival of Elena into her family is the trigger that shifts her. Her growth runs the length of the book.

## Habits
- Drinks tea (favours a particular Highland-edge tea room).
- Drives herself; will not be chauffeured unless the occasion requires it.
- Reads novels.
