# CASSIA VANCE — STATIC BIBLE

> Permanent facts about Cassia Vance.
> Read-only after Step 3b.

---

## Identity
- **Full name:** Cassia Vance
- **Title/Role:** sister of Roman Vance. Runs the Vance pack commercial portfolio. Member of the Wildwood Club. Elena's werewolf friend. Dedicated POV chapters in Arcs 8 and 9.
- **Age at story start:** 32
- **Unmated.**

## Physicality
- Similar height range to Roman's mother — around 5'8" or 5'9".
- Lean, athletic build. She runs as a wolf regularly.
- Dark brown hair (same family colouring as Roman), kept long.
- Grey eyes (a few shades lighter than Roman's pre-shift).
- Olive skin.

## Wolf form
- Smaller than Roman's but larger than the average wolf — Alpha-bloodline.
- Grey, with lighter markings on the belly and chest.

## Family
- Younger sister of Roman Vance (Roman is 38, Cassia 32).
- Daughter of Helena Vance and the late Alpha Vance (father deceased ten years before story start).
- Unmated — she has not found her mate. She is not actively looking and has stopped expecting.

## Position
- Runs the Vance pack commercial portfolio — significant family business holdings.
- A respected business operator in Highland.
- Owns a penthouse in central Highland District.
- Long-standing member of the Wildwood Club.
- Not a political operator. Marcus, not Cassia, is the one Roman turns to for territorial politics.

## Personality
- Sharp, quick, businesslike.
- Warmer than Roman but with the same teeth.
- Protective of her family.
- Initially skeptical of Elena. Comes to love her genuinely as the friendship develops.
- Does not suffer fools.

## Speech patterns
- Crisper than Roman's. She talks faster.
- Comfortable in social settings; comfortable in business settings.
- Dry humour.

## Internal voice (when her POV)
- Quick, appraising, warm underneath.
- Thinks in deals and reads. She measures people.
- Loves her brother and is wary of his happiness because she has never seen him be this happy.

## Position on Elena
- At story start, has not met Elena.
- After meeting her in Arc 2 (the mating party preparations and party), she becomes genuinely invested.
- By Arc 8 she folds Elena into her circle.
- By Arc 9 she is the bridge Elena uses to access werewolf high society on her own terms.
- The unresolved Cassia question (whether she realises Elena was using their outings as cover for Diego meetings) is open through the end of the book and may or may not be addressed explicitly.

## Relationship with Roman
- Sister and confidante. They are close.
- She will reprimand him when he deserves it.
- He reprimands her once in Arc 8 for taking Elena out without warning him.

## Habits
- Runs at the Wildwood Club two or three times a week.
- Drinks red wine.
- Owns multiple cars, drives them all.
