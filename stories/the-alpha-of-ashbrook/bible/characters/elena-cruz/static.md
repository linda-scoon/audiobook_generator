# ELENA CRUZ — STATIC BIBLE

> Permanent facts about Elena Cruz.
> Read-only after Step 3b.

---

## Identity
- **Full name:** Elena Cruz
- **Title/Role:** owner and operator of Cruz Community Fitness; resident of Ashbrook's South Side; female lead and POV character.
- **Age at story start:** 29
- **Second-generation Mexican-American.**

## Physicality
- Height 5'6". Athletic, strong, not lean-model thin. She trains.
- Warm brown skin.
- Dark brown almost-black hair, usually in a low ponytail.
- Dark brown eyes.
- Hands strong, sometimes chalk-chapped from training.
- Small scar above the right knee (childhood bike accident).

## Family of origin
- Father Hector Cruz, deceased. Heart attack when Elena was 22. Former high school PE teacher.
- Mother Marisol Cruz, late 50s, alive, runs a small Mexican bakery in the same neighbourhood as the gym.
- Older brother Diego Cruz, 34, contractor.
- Diego has his own family — wife Lucía and son Mateo.

## Background
- Born and raised in Ashbrook's South Side.
- Kinesiology + business degree from state university.
- Opened Cruz Community Fitness at 26.
- Has run the gym for three years at story start.
- Pre/post-natal fitness for human women is her main client base; she is locally known for this.
- After-school youth athletics program through the gym.
- General low-priced fitness classes.
- Two part-time instructors. Diego does maintenance for free.

## Speech patterns
- Brisk, gets to the point.
- Bilingual: Spanish with her mother and brother, English otherwise.
- Spanish phrases drift in when she is emotional.
- Curses freely when stressed or angry.
- Formal and precise when making a case to authority.
- When frightened or overwhelmed: goes very quiet.

## Internal voice
- Third-person close POV when her chapter.
- Sharp, alert, dry, slightly funny when not in danger.
- She has the eye of someone who has been the underdog her whole life. She clocks who has power and who doesn't in every room.
- Sensory specificity — she notices textures, smells, surfaces.

## Physical tells
- Bites the inside of her cheek when thinking or controlling anger. **RESERVE for high-tension moments.**
- Rotating cheap hair ties on her left wrist.
- Goes silent under real fear — no tears, no shaking, just stillness. This is her most dangerous mode.

## Habits
- Too much coffee. Black, sometimes with cinnamon.
- Owns one nice dress — a deep green wrap dress, her mother's gift — worn to community meetings.
- Reads paperback thrillers in bed to wind down.

## Pre-story romantic and sexual history
- Never been in a serious relationship.
- Too busy, too proud, and few suitable matches.
- She is sexually experienced but selective. Does not currently date.

## Political stance and history
- Skeptical of werewolves on principle. Not hateful. Not trusting.
- Considers mate bonds a werewolf problem.
- Knows the mate bond rules in the abstract (everyone in this country does) but has never expected to be one.
- Was an activist in her early twenties — petitions, organising community meetings, light protest work. Stepped back as the gym demanded her time but never stopped caring.
- Had been planning to leave the country before the story started. She had savings, friends planning to leave with her, an international destination. **Roman does not know this until Arc 18.**

## Pre-story relationship with the petition
- For three years before Ch 1, Elena petitioned the Alpha's office to convert the empty lot next to Cruz Community Fitness into a youth sports facility.
- Denied three times. Marcus Hale signed each denial on behalf of the Alpha's office.
- Roman never personally saw the petition until Ch 1.
- The petition is the reason she was in the hearing hall.
