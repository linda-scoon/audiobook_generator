# MARCUS HALE — STATIC BIBLE

> Permanent facts about Marcus Hale.
> Read-only after Step 3b.

---

## Identity
- **Full name:** Marcus Hale
- **Title/Role:** Beta of the Vance Pack. Roman's second. Political operator. Architect (with Roman) of the chess play with the two bills.
- **Age at story start:** 36

## Physicality
- Around 6 feet tall.
- Lean, athletic.
- Brown hair, neat.
- Hazel eyes.
- Kind face that has spent a lot of time saying *we'll review.*

## Family
- Married. Wife and children referenced; they do not appear on page.
- Pack family of long standing in the Vance territory.

## Position
- Beta to Roman for all 12 years of Roman's tenure.
- Runs the day-to-day operations of the Vance pack territory.
- Runs the quarterly South Side hearings on Roman's behalf (has done for 8 years).
- Signs the bulk of routine territorial decisions on Roman's behalf — including the three denials of Elena's petition before Ch 1.
- Voice of reason. Keeps Roman from territorial catastrophes.

## Personality
- Calm, dry-witted, patient.
- Pragmatic political operator.
- Loyal to Roman but willing to push back.
- He has the kind of face that lets people think he is on their side even when he is not.

## Speech patterns
- Even, low, professional.
- Says *Alpha* in public, *Roman* in private when familiar.
- His repeated phrase to petitioners: *we'll review.* It is the polite refusal that closes hearings.

## Internal voice
- He does not get a dedicated POV chapter. His interiority is shown only through what he says and does.

## Position on Elena
- Knows her petition history professionally. Signed the three denials.
- After the claiming, he is in damage-control mode managing optics.
- In Arc 12 (post-kidnap) he takes Elena aside and hints at the longer game. He is the one who plants the chess-play seed for her.
- He becomes a steady, careful presence around her — neither warm nor cold. Professional and protective.

## Relationship with Roman
- Beta and old friend.
- Will reprimand Roman when needed. Once in Arc 9 (the morning yoga interruption) he reprimands him for shirking territorial duties. The reprimand is jokingly framed but seriously meant.
- They are the architects of the bill strategy together.

## Habits
- Always has a tablet. Reads briefings constantly.
- Drinks coffee with milk.
