## Identity
- **Full name:** Roman Vance
- **Title:** Alpha of the Vance Pack
- **Age at story start:** 38

## Physicality
- Height 6'4". Broad-shouldered, dense muscle. Built like he uses it — not bulky.
- Dark brown hair, short, slight wave.
- Olive skin.
- Square jaw.
- Faint scar through the left eyebrow (from a fight at age 24).
- Eyes pale grey. Shift to gold when the wolf is close to the surface.

## Wolf form
- Massive grey wolf, near-black along the spine, gold eyes.
- Larger than an ordinary wolf — Alphas always are.

## Family of origin
- Father deceased (natural causes, ten years before story start).
- Mother Helena Vance, alive, lives at her own dowager estate twenty minutes outside the city.
- One younger sister, Cassia Vance, 32, unmated, runs the pack commercial portfolio.

## Position and history
- Alpha of the Vance Pack for 12 years. Won the alpha challenge at 26, the same week he inherited the estate.
- The Vance territory is one of the larger pack territories in the country. Includes the city of Ashbrook and surrounding counties.
- Holds a seat on the Alpha Council.
- Answers to no one above him within the country's political structure.

## Speech patterns
- Short sentences when commanding. Longer when persuading or remembering.
- Rarely raises his voice. The lower his voice gets, the more dangerous.
- Uses possessive language: my pack, my territory, mine.
- Under high emotion shifts into a slightly older, more formal register — almost archaic.
- Does not curse often. When he does, it lands.

## Internal voice
- Third-person close POV when his chapter.
- Measured, observational, controlled.
- The wolf is a constant low presence, surfacing in italicised intrusions. Short, urgent, animal.
- The wolf never speaks aloud.
- Roman the man calculates; the wolf wants. They are not always in agreement.

## Physical tells
- Pinches the bridge of his nose when controlling temper.
- When near Elena, traces wrist → pulse point → nape sequence to calm his wolf. **RESERVE for high-emotion moments only.**
- Eyes flash gold when the wolf is near the surface.
- The "lean" of his wolf — an attentive tilt of focus when Elena is near.

## Habits
- Black coffee, no sugar.
- Cooks his own breakfast when he can.
- Tailored dark suits for public events. Jeans and dark t-shirts at home.
- Whiskey neat. Bourbon, not Scotch.

## The Pull (specific to him)
- A physical sensation in his chest, like a tether tightening.
- When Elena is far away or distressed, the pull becomes a yank.
- When she is physically near, the pull calms but does not disappear.

## Romantic and sexual history before Elena
- Has had women throughout his adult life. A discreet rotation of pack women and the occasional human.
- Ends arrangements cleanly. No drama, no scandal.
- At 38 he has not found his mate. Stopped expecting her at 26, stopped thinking about her at 32.
- The absence has been quiet loneliness, not torment.

## Political stance
- Politically moderate by werewolf standards.
- Believes pack obligations extend to humans in his territory.
- Not a human-rights activist. Not a bigot. Fair within an unfair system.
- By the time the story starts, he has not personally investigated the conditions humans live in within his territory. He signs the policy denials his Beta puts in front of him.
