# DIEGO CRUZ — STATIC BIBLE

> Permanent facts about Diego Cruz.
> Read-only after Step 3b.

---

## Identity
- **Full name:** Diego Cruz
- **Title/Role:** Elena's older brother. Contractor by trade. Quiet community organiser, escalating to active resistance organiser during the back half of the story. Dedicated POV character across 10 chapters spread through Arcs 4–16.
- **Age at story start:** 34

## Physicality
- Big and broad. Around 6'1".
- Strong from a life of contracting work.
- Same warm brown skin and dark hair as Elena. Hair short, often unshaven.
- Brown eyes.
- Calloused hands. A small scar on the back of the left hand from a saw incident.

## Family
- Younger sister Elena Cruz, 29.
- Mother Marisol Cruz, late 50s.
- Father Hector Cruz, deceased.
- **Wife: Lucía Cruz.** Mid-thirties. Pragmatic, principled, terrified for their son.
- **Son: Mateo Cruz**, 6 or 7 years old. Has a wooden lion Diego carved for him at age 3.

## Background
- Born and raised in Ashbrook's South Side.
- Did not go to college.
- Has run his own contracting work for a decade. Modestly successful within the South Side economy.
- Has been a community presence — helping families, organising small protests, maintaining the gym for Elena.
- The activist work was always small until the events of Ch 1–9.

## Personality
- Protective. Fierce about his family.
- Quieter than Elena. Lets her do the talking.
- Practical, hands-on, problem-solver.
- Will not back down once he has decided something is wrong.
- Loves his son and wife in a way he tries not to show.

## Speech patterns
- Plainer than Elena's. Shorter sentences.
- Spanish drifts in for prayer and for his son.
- Does not curse much. When he does, it is purposeful.

## Internal voice (when his POV)
- Plain, practical, observational.
- He thinks in tasks and in faces, not in big abstractions.
- His love for his family is what shows when his guard is down.

## Position at story start
- Drove Elena to the hearing the morning of Ch 1. Sat in the truck with her. Said, *"Polite is a weapon, Elena."*
- In the hall when Roman claimed Elena. Lunged. Was put down by a Gamma. Got to the steps in time to see the car pull away.
- Currently on the hearing hall steps at end of Ch 9. Will go home from there.

## Trajectory across the book
- Arc 4 (Ch 30-44): receives the phone call from Roman. Tells Lucía. Begins organising more seriously.
- Arc 9 (Ch 85-138): leads the cell that burns the small government building successfully. Receives Elena's intel. Plans the documents heist.
- Arc 14: caught during the heist. Arrested.
- Arc 16: visited by Elena in detention. Beaten and tortured.
- Released after Elena's begging (Arc 16 end). Returns home broken but alive.
- Arc 17: meets Roman face-to-face when Roman comes to the South Side.
- Final image: present in the sports ground scene? Possibly. Bible does not require this.

## Habits
- Drinks American beer.
- Drives a contractor's truck.
